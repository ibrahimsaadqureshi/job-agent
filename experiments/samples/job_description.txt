ABOUT MERIDIAN AI

Meridian AI is a Series A startup building intelligent workflow automation for mid-market revenue teams. We help companies replace manual CRM hygiene, lead routing, and onboarding workflows with AI-native pipelines. Our customers include SaaS companies, agencies, and operations-heavy teams across North America and Europe.

We are a team of 34 people, fully remote, backed by $12M in Series A funding.

---

THE ROLE

We are looking for an AI Automation & Solutions Engineer to sit at the intersection of pre-sales, implementation, and customer success. You will work directly with new customers to configure, deploy, and optimize Meridian AI for their existing tech stack.

This is a high-ownership role. You will be the technical voice in sales calls, the person who builds the first automation for a new customer, and the internal advocate who translates customer feedback into product improvements.

---

WHAT YOU WILL DO

- Lead technical discovery calls with prospects to understand their CRM workflows, data structure, and automation goals
- Design and implement AI-powered automation workflows using Meridian AI, n8n, and customer-owned tools
- Integrate Meridian AI with HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, Pipedrive, and other CRM platforms via REST APIs and webhooks
- Build onboarding playbooks and configuration templates for repeatable customer segments
- Identify automation gaps and work with the product team to prioritize integrations
- Monitor deployed workflows, troubleshoot failures, and optimize performance
- Document implementation patterns for internal reuse and customer-facing knowledge base
- Occasionally support pre-sales demos for technical prospects

---

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

Required:
- 2+ years of experience in a solutions engineering, implementation, technical operations, or automation role
- Hands-on experience with at least one CRM platform (HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, or similar)
- Experience building workflows in n8n, Zapier, Make, or a comparable automation platform
- Comfort working with REST APIs, webhooks, and JSON data structures
- Strong written communication — you will write technical documentation and async customer updates regularly
- Ability to manage multiple implementations simultaneously without losing detail

Preferred:
- Experience with AI tooling — prompt engineering, LLM API integration, or AI-powered workflow design
- Familiarity with Python for scripting, data transformation, or API interaction
- Exposure to RevOps concepts: lead scoring, pipeline hygiene, lifecycle stage management
- Experience working in an early-stage startup environment
